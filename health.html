- Diet and Supplements - Raymond Peat - Experiments
My main health goals revolve around improving my quality of life, length of life and my ability to think creatively and solve interesting problems. I tend to avoid any kind of strict protocol unless it is part of a specific experiment that I am undertaking. My reasoning for this is that while strict protocols can have a place they should be implemented with care as they seem to reduce freedom and the abilty to think creatively.
